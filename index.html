Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any active subscription linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. Find Gratitude 3 and tap Cancel Subscription.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No. All your journal entries, mood data, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal content.

Can I export my journal?

PDF export is available with Gratitude 3 Pro. Go to Settings to upgrade and access this feature.

I lost my entries after reinstalling the app.

Since all data is stored locally on your device, reinstalling the app will remove your entries. We recommend regularly backing up your device via iCloud.
